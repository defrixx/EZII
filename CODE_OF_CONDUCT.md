# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code and outcomes, not people.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and de-escalate conflicts.
- Accept accountability and correct mistakes quickly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, deliberate provocation, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit consent.
- Sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct and may restrict participation as needed to protect the community.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers (use direct maintainer contact on GitHub, not a public issue).

All reports are reviewed confidentially and handled as promptly as possible.
